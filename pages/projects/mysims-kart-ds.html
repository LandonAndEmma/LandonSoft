MySims Kart DS

A Mario Kart DS ROM hack featuring an entirely new experience with MySims characters.

Features

  • 32 brand new courses
  • Complete roster replacement with MySims characters
  • Custom graphics and theming

This ambitious ROM hack transforms Mario Kart DS into a MySims racing experience, featuring completely redesigned courses and a unique roster of beloved MySims characters.
